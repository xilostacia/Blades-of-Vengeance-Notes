**Type:** Island Nation  
**Capital:** [[Yamashiro]]  
**Government:** Shōgunate  
**Legendary Shōgun:** [[Miyamoto Kurosawa]]  
**Current Shōgun:** [[Yorinobu Kurosawa]]

## Overview

Island nation far east across the [[Sea of Tears]]. Surrounded by volcanic peaks and perpetual fog. Recently opened to foreign trade after centuries of isolation.

# Feudal Hierarchy of Cipangu

## Apex: Samurai Aristocracy

- Governed by bushidō
- Serve the Shōgun
- Rule through daimyō

## Shōgun

Supreme ruler of Cipangu.
Succession:
- Traditionally hereditary (father to son)
- If no heir, daimyō select successor

## Daimyō

Clan lords.
- Govern cities and castles
- Command ashigaru armies

## Ashigaru

Foot soldiers.
- Increasingly armed with matchlock arquebuses

## Religious Class

Monks and clerics.
- Maintain spiritual balance
- Mediators between mortal and unseen realms

## Commoners

Farmers, artisans, fishermen, merchants.
- Sustain society
- Expected to serve as ashigaru during wa
