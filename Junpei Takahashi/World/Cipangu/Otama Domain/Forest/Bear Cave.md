**Type:** Cave  
**Location:** Forest outside [[Kaido Hana]]

## Discovery
Located approximately **one mile from the destroyed farmhouse**.
An **overturned cart** was found outside the cave that appeared to have been dragged there and ransacked for food.

## Interior
Inside the cave were:
- Old blood
- Yellowed animal bones
- Some human remains

The cave splits into **two passages**:
- One ends shortly
- One leads deeper

## Creatures Found
Two large bears occupied the cave.

### First Bear
Killed by the party after being grappled by [[Raiden]].

### Second Bear
Appeared immediately after the first bear was killed.  
Likely the mate of the first.

This bear proved far more difficult to kill and repeatedly rose after being struck down.

Eventually slain by the group.
