Also called ninja.

## Role
- Espionage
- Assassination
- Sabotage
- Silent warfare

## Employers
- Daimyo
- Merchant guilds
- Monasteries

## Cultural Position

Necessary but dishonorable.  
Operate outside bushidō.

## Lore

- Ninjutsu originates from [[Yōkai]]
- Techniques recorded in coded manuals
- Blend of mortal skill and supernatural power
