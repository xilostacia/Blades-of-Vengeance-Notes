**Location:** [[Kaido Hana]], [[Otama Domain]], [[Cipangu]]
# Timeline
---
## Kabuki Theatre
While attending the kabuki performance, I encountered a **kame** (tortle) named **Raiden**.
- Around **6+ feet tall**
- Likely **close to 500 lbs**

Raiden asked for food after sitting next to me in the kabuki audience.  
A **samurai sitting to my right** gave him a rice ball.

The performance began shortly after.

---
## The Kabuki Performance
A **single kabuki performer** came onto the stage and began a monologue about the lands.

During the performance:
- A **silver dragon** appeared.
- The performer explained that **samurai keep the land safe**.
- A **samurai and three oni** appeared.

The samurai leapt forward and **cut off all three oni heads**.

I noticed something unusual:

The **oni heads (masks)** were **floating in the air**, not attached to actors.  
A simple but effective stage illusion.

The dragon vanished.

The performer ended the scene saying:

> The dragon will return when the lands need him again.

---
## The Interruption
Suddenly the **doors burst open**, interrupting the performance.

A man entered:
- Sweating
- Emaciated
- Collapsed immediately

He cried out for **help**.

When he fell over his **back was visibly cut open and ravaged**.

---
## Healing
Two people approached him:
- [[Raiden]] (the kame)
- A **kabuki actor in the audience** who was **not performing**

The kabuki actor **healed the man with magic**.

---
## Theatre Master

Another man appeared, identifying himself as the **master of the theatre**.

**Name:** [[Hiko]]

At this point I:
- Turned from the stage toward the aisle
- Remained seated
- Drew a **kukri**
- Held it hidden inside my sleeve

I did **not stand**.

---
## The Survivor
The wounded man regained consciousness briefly.

He began screaming repeatedly about **a bear**.

He continued yelling until **Raiden restrained him** to prevent him hurting himself.

Shortly after, the man **passed out again**.

---
## Backstage
The man was **dragged backstage** to the performer area.

Those assisting him:
- [[Raiden]]
- the kabuki actor
- the samurai

They intended to stabilize and treat him.

I remained seated to see whether the performance would continue.

---
## Leaving the Theatre
Eventually I left the theatre along with the rest of the audience.

Shortly afterward the following exited as well:
- [[Raiden]]
- the **samurai**
- the **kabuki actor**

They discussed **going after the bear**.

I approached them and asked **why they were going to fight a bear**.

Raiden was **very eager**, saying it would be **a good fight**.

---
## Introductions
The group:
- [[Raiden]] — kame
- [[Takara Hideyoshi]] — kabuki actor
- **"Swordsman"** — samurai (refused another name)

I introduced myself simply as **Takahashi**.

---
## The Headman's Manor
We traveled to the **Headman's Manor**, the **second largest building in town**.

At the entrance we were greeted by a **small girl in a pink kimono**.

**Name:** [[Kairi]]

She went to retrieve her father.

---
## The Headman
We were brought into a side room.

An older man (~50 years old) was **playing a tile game**.

He introduced himself as:

**[[Takeshi]] — Headman**

---
## The Bear Story
The injured man had reported:
- A **large scarred brown bear**
- About **one day's travel outside town**

Kairi mentioned a **“Kazagake Bear”**.

Takeshi explained this was **an old story from several hundred years ago**.

---
## The Job
We were offered **50 gold each** for **proof the large bear exists**.

Takeshi himself **does not believe the story**.

---
## Leaving Town
We left [[Kaido Hana]] and entered the forest.

Raiden led the group using the map.

---
## Bandit Ambush
After several hours we encountered:
- A **wall of downed bamboo**

I recognized it as **an ambush**.

A man with a **rifle** emerged demanding coin.

There were **six other men**.

I warned the others and moved into the bamboo to attack.

---
## Fight with the Bandits

During the fight:
- The bandit leader's **gun exploded**
- He was badly injured

I told him to **surrender**.

He did.

The others **robbed him**, after [[Takara Hideyoshi]] cut the tendons ont he back of his hand so he could not open them again.

---
## Continuing the Journey
We took a short break at the bandit camp.

Nothing particularly valuable was found.

We continued deeper toward the location.

---
## Nightfall
As evening approached we prepared to make camp.

We discovered **a massive paw print**.

Size: **roughly the size of a human torso**

It is **definitely from the bear**.
