**Location:** Forest outside [[Kaido Hana]], [[Otama Domain]], [[Cipangu]]

---
# Timeline

## Following the Bear Tracks

The group continued following the **bear tracks** deeper into the forest.

After some time the terrain became a denser forest.  
I detected the **heavy scent of blood**, from both a **male and female** source.

I did not mention this to the others since we were already heading in roughly the correct direction.

---
## The Cabin

[[Takara Hideyoshi]] led us slightly away from the tracks toward the smell of blood.
We eventually discovered:
- A **small house**
- A **wagon**
- An **ox slaughtered in its pen**

The **front of the house had been torn apart**.

Inside:
- The bodies of what appeared to be the **man's family**
- A **dog**
- Large amounts of blood

The dog was a **wounded Akita**.

There was also a **trail of bloody footprints**, likely belonging to the injured man who escaped earlier.

---
## The Dog

The dog's name tag read **[[Koko]]**.
Observations:
- Did not like [[Raiden]]
- Approached [[Takara Hideyoshi]] after he gave it dried meat

The dog appeared to want us to **follow the bear's trail**.

---
## The Cave
I climbed onto the roof of the house to survey the area.
In the direction the dog indicated I saw:
- A **cave roughly a mile away**
- An **overturned cart**

We traveled toward that location.

---
## The Cart
The cart appeared to have been **dragged to the cave**.

It had been **ransacked for food**.

---
## Inside the Cave
The cave interior was:
- Dark
- Covered in **old blood**
- Filled with **yellowed bones of animals**
- Containing **some human remains**

The cave split into two directions.
- One path ended shortly
- The other led deeper

Following the deeper passage we found the **bear**.

The bear was among:
- The corpses of **two horses**
- Large amounts of blood and gore

---
## First Bear Fight
[[Raiden]] approached the bear.
Positions were taken:
- Raiden engaged directly
- [[Takara Hideyoshi]] moved aggressively
- [[Swordsman]] readyied himself but let the the other two fight
- I stayed back at distance

I drew my **hunting bow** to support if needed.

### Combat
The fight began as expected.
Events during the fight:
- Raiden grappled the bear
- Takara leapt over Raiden
- Takara forced a **heated spearhead into the bear's mouth**
The fight ended quickly.
Raiden finished the bear.

---
## The Second Bear
After the victory roar from Raiden, another roar answered.
This was the **second bear**, apparently the **mate of the first**.
The bear charged in a rage.

---
## Second Fight
I remained behind a **rock pillar** and fired arrows while the others engaged the bear directly.
### Arrows Fired
- 2 hits
- 2 misses

During the fight:
- Raiden took severe damage
- He was knocked unconscious and fell onto his shell

The bear repeatedly rose after being struck down multiple times.

It was finally defeated after being **overwhelmed by the group**.

---
## After the Fight
The dog **Koko**, who had remained outside the cave, survived.
Near the cave entrance we found:
- A **bloody note**
- A **small pack**

The note was written by **[[Hero Tanaka]]**.

---
## Return to Camp
We returned to the **bandit camp** from earlier.
We rested there for the night.
During the night I explained what I knew about **Hero Tanaka**, the man I had previously been mistaken for.

---
## Morning
Near morning I was awakened by [[Takara Hideyoshi]].
I performed some **light exercise** while food was prepared.
After the others woke, we began traveling back toward [[Kaido Hana]].

---
## End of Session

We approached the **gates of Kaido Hana** in the distance.
